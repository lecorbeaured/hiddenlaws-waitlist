Who We Are

This website is operated by Eric Coste, an independent author and publisher based in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. References to "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to Eric Coste.

This policy applies to the website where you are reading this, any associated email list, and digital products sold or distributed under the Eric Coste name.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: hello@ericcoste.com

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to deliver the service you requested.

Information you provide directly:

  • First name — used to personalise emails sent to you
  • Email address — used to send the free series and any future communications you have opted into
  • Contact form submissions — name, email, and message content when you use the contact form

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address — collected by hosting and analytics providers for security and basic traffic reporting
  • Browser and device type — used to ensure the site renders correctly
  • Pages visited and time on page — collected in aggregate to understand which content is most useful
  • Referral source — which site or link brought you here

We do not collect payment information directly. All purchases are processed by third-party payment providers such as Payhip and Stripe, which have their own privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Delivering the free email series you opted into
  • Sending follow-up emails related to the content you signed up for
  • Notifying you of new books, guides, or related resources from Eric Coste that may be relevant to you
  • Responding to messages sent through the contact form
  • Improving the website and email content based on aggregate usage data
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not use your information for automated profiling, targeted advertising, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

Email Communications

By submitting your email address on this site, you are opting in to receive the free 3-part series and occasional emails from Eric Coste. These may include follow-up content, book announcements, and relevant resources.

You can unsubscribe at any time. Every email we send includes a clearly visible unsubscribe link at the bottom. Clicking it immediately removes you from the list. You will not receive further emails after unsubscribing.

We use Resend to deliver email. Your email address and first name are stored in Resend's system. Resend's privacy policy is available at resend.com/privacy.

We do not send spam. We do not share your email address with advertisers or any third party for marketing purposes. Ever.

  • Resend — email delivery. Privacy policy
  • Netlify — website hosting and infrastructure. Privacy policy
  • Payhip — digital product sales and delivery. Privacy policy
  • Stripe — payment processing (via Payhip). Privacy policy

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

Cookies and Analytics

This site uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or fingerprinting technologies.

If basic analytics are enabled, data is collected in aggregate and anonymised. No individual user profiles are built. You can disable cookies entirely in your browser settings without losing access to any content on this site.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any behavioural advertising platform on this site.

Data Retention

We retain your email address and name for as long as you remain subscribed to the email list. When you unsubscribe, your data is removed from active sending lists immediately. Residual copies in backup systems are purged within 30 days.

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months for the purpose of reference and follow-up, then deleted.

Purchase records held by Payhip and Stripe are subject to their own retention policies and applicable financial regulations.

Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request that your personal data be deleted
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to how your data is being processed
  • Withdrawal of consent — unsubscribe or withdraw consent at any time without penalty

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@ericcoste.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information), and to request deletion. Contact us at the email above to exercise these rights.

EU / UK residents (GDPR): Our lawful basis for processing your email address is consent, which you provided when you submitted the opt-in form. You may withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing. If you believe your rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encrypted connections (HTTPS), restricted access to data systems, and use of reputable third-party infrastructure providers.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we use industry-standard protections, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

This site and its content are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us at hello@ericcoste.com and we will delete it promptly.

Continued use of this site or the email list after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
